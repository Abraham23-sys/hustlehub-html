The process

How HustleHub works

Three steps, no middlemen, no commission taken from your hustle.

  1. Create a listing

    Post what you offer, your rate, and where you're reachable on campus.

  2. Get discovered

    Students search the marketplace by category when they need your service.

  3. Agree & deliver

    Chat directly, agree on terms, and get paid — HustleHub never takes a cut.

Before you start

Frequently asked questions

Is it free to post a hustle?

Yes. HustleHub does not charge listing fees or take a commission on what you earn.

Can I list more than one hustle?

Yes, use the registration form to add each service separately so it appears under the right category.

How do buyers contact me?

Your listing shows the contact details you choose to share when you register.
